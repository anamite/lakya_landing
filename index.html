Our Core Programmes

Your Path to Germany Starts Here

We offer a curated range of Germany-specific pathways — each with dedicated support, documentation guidance and placement assistance.

Most Popular

Ausbildung

Germany's renowned dual vocational training system — earn while you learn. Ausbildung combines hands-on workplace training with classroom education, leading to a recognised German qualification and a career path in Germany.

  • Paid training — earn €700–€1,200/month from Day 1
  • Recognised German qualification upon completion
  • Wide range of fields: nursing, IT, hospitality & more
  • Path to permanent residency in Germany
Enquire About Ausbildung →
Academic

Masters Degree

Pursue a world-class Master's degree at one of Germany's prestigious public universities — most of which offer tuition-free education even for international students. Elevate your career with a globally recognised German qualification.

  • Mostly tuition-free at public universities
  • English and German-taught programmes available
  • Blocked account & health insurance guidance included
  • 18-month job seeker visa after graduation
Explore Masters Options →
Volunteer

FSJ & BFD Programmes

FSJ (Freiwilliges Soziales Jahr) and BFD (Bundesfreiwilligendienst) are Germany's prestigious voluntary service programmes. Give back to society while building your German language skills, network and future career prospects.

  • Open to international applicants aged 18–26 (FSJ)
  • Monthly allowance + accommodation provided
  • Learn German on the job — full language integration
  • Excellent foundation before Ausbildung or studies
Learn About FSJ & BFD →
Cultural Exchange

Au Pair Programme

Join a trusted German host family as an Au Pair — experience German culture from the inside, improve your language skills and receive accommodation, meals and a monthly stipend. LAKYA carefully matches you with verified, welcoming families.

  • Trusted, pre-screened German host families
  • Monthly pocket money + full board & lodging
  • Max 30 working hours per week
  • Ideal bridge before studies or Ausbildung

Undergraduate

Bachelor's Degree

Begin your academic journey in Germany with a fully-recognised Bachelor's degree programme. Germany's universities offer cutting-edge education in engineering, medicine, business, arts and more — at minimal to zero tuition cost.

  • Tuition-free at most public German universities
  • APS certificate guidance included
  • Wide range of English-taught programmes
  • Full admission and enrolment support

Additional Services

Everything You Need, Under One Roof

Beyond placement, LAKYA provides a full suite of support services to ensure your journey to Germany is smooth, legal and stress-free.

Documentation Support

We guide you through every document required for your application — from academic certificates to financial proofs — ensuring nothing is missed and everything is correctly formatted for German authorities.

Certified Translations

Professional translation of your academic transcripts, birth certificates, police clearance certificates and any other documents — certified and accepted by German embassies and institutions.

Visa Processing Assistance

We prepare and review your complete visa application, help with blocked account setup, health insurance arrangements, and embassy appointment booking — maximising your approval chances.

Career Guidance

Not sure which path is right for you? Our experienced advisors help you identify your strengths, explore German career options and select the programme best aligned with your goals and background.

Airport Pickup

Arriving in Germany alone can feel overwhelming. LAKYA arranges a warm, reliable airport pickup to receive you personally — ensuring your very first hours in Germany feel safe, comfortable and welcoming.

Accommodation Guidance

We help you find suitable, affordable housing options near your training facility or university — whether it's a student dormitory, shared flat (WG) or temporary accommodation during your first weeks.

German Language Prep

We connect you with trusted German language course providers and resources to help you reach the required proficiency level (A1–B2) before your departure — giving you the confidence to thrive from day one.

Post-Arrival Support

Our support doesn't end at the airport. We assist with city registration (Anmeldung), bank account opening, health insurance activation and orientation — so you settle in quickly and confidently.
